About

I was born in the Philippines and raised primarily in the beautiful state of Kentucky. My amazing partner, Ashley, & I share our lives with two beautiful children, Elijah & June.

We've enjoyed establishing our family's roots in the city of Louisville, and we've come to enjoy its diverse and cosmopolitan aspects. From exploring its insanely good local dining scene to enjoying its pull of solid entertainment options, we've truly come to love this town.

Living in Louisville, I've been grateful for easy access to a state filled with seeemingly endless natural beauty. Opportunities to hike and explore, with my family, is truly one of my greatest joys. Our family's ongoing project to visit every waterfall that we can find in Kentucky has been a gift unto itself.

Thanks for spending a bit of your time to know a little about me. Feel free to send me a message on my Connect page, if you'd like to know more.

Experience

I've spent most of my working life in the restaurant industry and customer service. I've worked in all aspects of front-of-house service, and, in most recent years, I've primarily served in a management role. I made the transition into the tech industry, early in 2022, and I'm currently employed in technical support for point-of-sale systems offered by Global Payments in Jeffersonville, Indiana.

I'm excited to bring a strong foundation in customer service and effective team-based dynamics into a technologically-driven environment. I'm passionate about the tech industry and look forward to continuing to hone and broaden my skills.
