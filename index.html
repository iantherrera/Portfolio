About

I was born in the Philippines and raised primarily in the beautiful state of Kentucky. With the support of my amazing partner, Ashley, I have been blessed with the privilege of raising two beautiful children, Elijah & June, and I enjoy sharing as much of my time as possible with them.

Having established roots in the city of Louisville, we take every opportunity to enjoy its cosmopolitan aspects. From exploring its multitudes of local dining & entertainment options, as well as its rich local cultural events, we look forward to constantly seeking new adventures around every corner of this vibrant city.

Living in a state full of natural beauty, we also enjoy exploring, camping & hiking, particularly in the Red River Gorge & Daniel Boone National Forest. Our ongoing project to visit every waterfall in Kentucky has gifted us with discovering many of its seemingly endless natural wonders.

Thank you for taking a bit of your time to get to know a little about me. Feel free to send me a message on my Connect page, if you'd like to know more!

Experience

I've spent most of my working life in the restaurant industry and customer service. I've worked in all aspects of front-of-house service, and, in most recent years, I've primarily served in a Management role. I made the transition into the tech industry, early in 2022, and I'm currently employed in direct technical support for point-of-sale products offered by Global Payments, in Jeffersonville, Indiana.

I'm excited to bring a strong foundation in customer service and team-based dynamics into a technologically-driven environment. I'm passionate and motivated about the tech industry and look forward to continually honing and broadening my development skills to offer myself as a valuable asset and team member to any organization.
